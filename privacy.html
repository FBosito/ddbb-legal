Privacy Policy

DDBB Stats Agent · Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the DDBB Stats Agent application ("the App"), operated by Drum Dad & Bass Boy ("we", "us", "our"), handles data. The App is a private analytics tool used to monitor our own social media performance.

2. Data We Collect

The App collects only our own account performance metrics from the TikTok API, including:

  • Follower count
  • Video view counts
  • Like, comment, and share counts
  • Engagement rates

We do not collect any personal data from other TikTok users.

3. How We Use the Data

All collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Monitoring our own social media performance
  • Tracking growth over time
  • Comparing metrics with industry benchmarks

4. Data Storage

Analytics data is stored locally and is not shared with any third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the TikTok API to retrieve account metrics. Our use of this API is governed by TikTok's Developer Terms and Privacy Policy.

6. User Rights

As the App does not collect or process personal data from external users, GDPR data subject requests are not applicable. However, if you have any questions, you may contact us.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children and does not collect any data from minors.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
